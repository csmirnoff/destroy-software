DestroY MediaPlay

Finally I'm officialy announcing DestroY MediaPlay, my new media player :-)) Release date: 4th Qtr 2003 / 1st Qtr 2004.
My intenttion is not to compete with other great media players neither with commercial ones.. but I want to try my best and try to get as near as possible to the "big media players".
This is the list of features I have planned for DestroY MediaPlay:

Features:
************

- Playback of .avi, .asf, .asx, .rmi, .wav, .wma, .wax, .wmv, .ogg, .mpg, .mpeg, .m1v, .mp2, .mp3, .mpa, .mpe , .mid, .rmi , .qt, .aif, .aifc, .aiff, .mov .au, .snd
- Audio CD Playback
- Internet streaming (audio & video)
- Complete Playlist reading/saving including well known list formats
- Add files by a button or by drag n' drop!
- Playlist HTML generator!
- Recursive file search engine
- 3 different Playlist views! (Path/Name/ID3)
- MP3/OGG/WAV and media information
- ID3 v1, ID3 v2 and OGG tag reading, editing and saving
- DirectX Sound Filters : Echo, Reverb, Flanger
- 10 bands DirectX Equalizer
- Sound Position Analog Meter
- Sound Volume Analog Knob
- Complete built-in sound mixer with sliders (volume & record devices)
- Volume and tracker sliders
- Full skins support!!
- Mini Internet browser
- Auto updater
- Complete system information (CPU, memory, Windows, Direct X, display settings, etc)
- File repeat, editable media speed rate and other functions
- Minimize to tray or taskbar
- Ability to control it from the systray
- Association to media files
- Right click menu with several features
- Choose to have it always on top or not
- Snap to screen edges function
- and much more!!...

DestroY MediaPlay will be FREEWARE!! Yes, you heard right! :D
