03.12.07
Tamerlane wins MineSweeper Online Tournament!
 


Tamerlane, from Canada, is the first Champion of the MineSweeper Online Tournament, after winning the Final Match against DestroY. Kimster, meanwhile, took 3rd place from Ventureraider.

Champion Tamerlane started the Tournament with a strong win against By Val. In his second match, however, Chiboring402 managed to beat him in a close game. Another win, against Sniek, guaranteed the Canadian his passport to the Playoffs.
In the Final Round, Tamerlane didn't lose any game, winning all of them against By Val in the Quarter Finals, Kimster in the Semi-Final, and DestroY, in the Final match. Congratulations!

01.13.07
MineSweeper Online Tournament announced!
 

You can now subscribe to the MineSweeper Online Tournament!

Have fun playing with players from all over the world in thrilling MineSweeper matches! All you need is an Internet connection and the desire to play and have fun in a healthy competition!

Some facts:
- Matches will be done only once a week at the date and time that both players arrange. It will take about 10 or 15 minutes a week and some exceptions to the time frame can be made.
- Please do not subscribe if you are unsure if you will be able to participate.
- The winner will get a special "Champion's Edition" of MineSweeper Online and will be added to the "Hall of Fame".

Do you want to subscribe? You can read the Rules and subscription instructions here! Subscription deadline and tournament start date will be announced soon!
